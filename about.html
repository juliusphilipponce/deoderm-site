Our Core Philosophy

"Dr. Deo Wong started this clinic with two non-negotiable advocacies: Dermatology must be inclusive, and it must be effective without being complicated."

Radical Inclusivity

"Ang dermatology ay para sa lahat." We are a safe space for every body, skin type, and gender identity. Our staff is trained to provide sensitive, affirmative care to everyone who walks through our doors.

Clinical Simplicity

"Keep it simple." We reject fear-mongering and over-prescription. We focus on evidence-based treatments that work, educating you on how to maintain your skin health long after you leave.
